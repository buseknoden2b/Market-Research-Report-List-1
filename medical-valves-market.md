<p><strong>The Medical Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One Way Valve</li><li>Multi Way Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical valves market primarily includes one-way and multi-way valves, essential for various medical applications. One-way valves allow fluid flow in a single direction, preventing backflow and ensuring safe administration of medications and fluids. Multi-way valves, on the other hand, enable the control of fluid flow through multiple pathways, facilitating complex procedures and treatment approaches. Both types are critical in maintaining system integrity and patient safety in medical devices like infusion systems and respiratory equipment.</p></p>
