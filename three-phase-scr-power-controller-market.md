<p><ul><li>Phase Angle Control</li><li>Integral Cycle Switching</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Three Phase SCR Power Controller market features two primary types: Phase Angle Control and Integral Cycle Switching. </p><p>Phase Angle Control adjusts the firing angle of silicon-controlled rectifiers (SCRs) to regulate power output continuously, allowing for precise control over electrical loads. </p><p>Integral Cycle Switching, on the other hand, switches the power on and off for entire cycles, providing an average voltage that can efficiently reduce heat and power consumption. Both methods cater to various industrial applications, offering flexibility in power management.</p></p>
