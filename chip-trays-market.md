<p><p>Chip trays are categorized into several market types based on material composition and properties. MPPE (Modified Polypropylene) offers durability and resistance to chemicals, making it effective for food packaging. PES (Polyethersulfone) is known for its high-temperature stability and is suitable for electronics. PS (Polystyrene) is widely used due to its efficiency in mass production and low cost. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) provides impact resistance and is ideal for rugged applications. Other materials may include bioplastics or composites, catering to specialized needs.</p></p>
