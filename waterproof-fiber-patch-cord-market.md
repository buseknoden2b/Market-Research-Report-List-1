<p><p>The Waterproof Fiber Patch Cord market comprises two main types: Single-mode and Multimode Fiber Optical Patch Cords. Single-mode fibers are designed for long-distance communication with a single light path, offering high bandwidth and minimal signal loss, ideal for extended networks. In contrast, Multimode fibers support multiple light paths, allowing for shorter distances and higher data rates, making them suitable for local area networks. Both types are engineered for durability and moisture resistance, ensuring reliable performance in harsh environments.</p></p>
