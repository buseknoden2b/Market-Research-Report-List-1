<p><p>The Audio IC market comprises three main segments: audio processors, audio amplifiers, and MEMS microphones. Audio processors manage and enhance sound signals, enabling features like noise reduction and equalization. Audio amplifiers boost audio signals for better sound output, catering to a range of devices from smartphones to home theaters. MEMS microphones are compact, high-fidelity microphones that utilize Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems technology, offering superior sound capture in a tiny form factor, making them ideal for portable electronics and smart devices.</p></p>
