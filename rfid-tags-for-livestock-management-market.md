<p><p>RFID tags for livestock management come in two main types: active and passive. Active RFID tags are battery-powered, enabling them to transmit data over long distances and store larger amounts of information. They are beneficial for tracking livestock in extensive areas. In contrast, passive RFID tags lack a battery and rely on energy from the reader to transmit information. They are typically more cost-effective and suitable for close-range identification. Both types enhance livestock tracking, health monitoring, and inventory management.</p></p>
