<p><p>Standard reed switches are essential components in various applications, available in power ratings like 3W and 20W. The market features two main types: Normally Open (NO) and Normally Closed (NC). Normally Open switches only complete the circuit when a magnetic field is present, ideal for safety applications. In contrast, Normally Closed switches maintain a closed circuit until influenced by a magnetic field, making them suitable for alarm systems. Both types are widely used in industries such as automotive, telecommunications, and home automation.</p></p>
