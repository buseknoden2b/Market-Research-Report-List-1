<p><p>The sodium silicate powder market is categorized into two main types: hydrous and anhydrous sodium silicate. Hydrous sodium silicate contains water molecules and is commonly used in applications such as detergents, adhesives, and sealants due to its binding properties. Anhydrous sodium silicate, on the other hand, lacks water and offers higher stability, making it suitable for applications in construction, ceramics, and automotive industries. Both types serve distinct functions tailored to specific industrial requirements, enhancing their market demand.</p></p>
