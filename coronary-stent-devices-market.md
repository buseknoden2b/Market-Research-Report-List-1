<p><strong>The Coronary Stent Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bare-Metal Stents</li><li>Drug-Eluting Stents</li><li>Bioabsorbable Stents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coronary stent devices market comprises three primary types: Bare-Metal Stents (BMS), which are basic mesh tubes providing structural support without drug coating; Drug-Eluting Stents (DES), which are coated with medication to prevent restenosis by releasing drugs gradually; and Bioabsorbable Stents, designed to dissolve over time, leaving no permanent implant. Each type serves specific patient needs, with BMS typically used for uncomplicated cases, DES for higher risk of restenosis, and bioabsorbable stents for those seeking temporary support.</p></p>
