<p><p>The supermarket refrigeration equipment market is primarily categorized into plug-in and remote systems. Plug-in refrigeration units are self-contained, easy to install, and typically used for smaller display cases or freezers, making them ideal for smaller retail environments. In contrast, remote refrigeration systems consist of separate components, where the compressor is located away from the display unit, providing greater energy efficiency and flexibility in larger supermarkets. Both types serve critical roles in preserving perishable goods while enhancing shopping experiences.</p></p>
