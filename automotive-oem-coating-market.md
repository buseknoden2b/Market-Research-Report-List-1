<p><p>The automotive OEM coating market consists of several types, including solvent-borne, waterborne, powdered, and others. Solvent-borne coatings utilize organic solvents for application, offering excellent finish and durability but raising VOC concerns. Waterborne coatings are environmentally friendly, using water as a primary solvent, resulting in lower emissions. Powdered coatings are applied as dry powders that cure upon heating, providing robust finishes and minimal waste. Other types may include high-solids and UV-curable coatings, catering to specific performance requirements and applications.</p></p>
