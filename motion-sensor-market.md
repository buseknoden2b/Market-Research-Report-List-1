<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic</li><li>Infra-Red (IR)</li><li>Microwave (MRW)</li><li>Tomographic</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motion sensor market includes various types, each using different technologies for detecting movement. Ultrasonic sensors emit sound waves to detect objects based on the time it takes for the waves to return. Infra-Red (IR) sensors rely on heat emitted by bodies to trigger detection. Microwave sensors use radio waves, offering greater range and penetration through obstacles. Tomographic sensors create a 3D image of the monitored area to detect movement. Other types may include capacitive and photoelectric sensors, catering to diverse applications.</p></p>
