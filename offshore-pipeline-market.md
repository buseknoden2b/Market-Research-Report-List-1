<p><p>The offshore pipeline market is primarily categorized into export lines and transport lines. Export lines are designed to transport hydrocarbons from offshore production facilities to onshore processing or distribution points, facilitating the global trade of oil and gas. Transport lines, on the other hand, focus on moving hydrocarbons between various offshore facilities, such as from production platforms to storage or processing locations. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring efficient resource management and energy supply in offshore oil and gas operations.</p></p>
