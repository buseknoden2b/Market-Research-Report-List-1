<p><p>The solar inverter market consists of three main types: Central Solar Inverters, String Solar Inverters, and Micro Solar Inverters. Central solar inverters are designed for large-scale solar power plants, converting energy from multiple arrays into grid-compatible electricity. String solar inverters connect several solar panels in series, making them suitable for residential and small commercial systems. Micro solar inverters, installed on individual panels, enhance efficiency by optimizing output at the panel level, making them ideal for complex roof installations with shading issues.</p></p>
