<p><strong>The High-Voltage Submarine Transmission Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DC Submarine Transmission Cable</li><li>AC Submarine Transmission Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-voltage submarine transmission cable market consists primarily of two types: DC (Direct Current) and AC (Alternating Current) submarine transmission cables. DC cables are used for long-distance power transmission, offering higher efficiency and reduced energy loss, making them suitable for connecting offshore wind farms to the grid. In contrast, AC cables are typically employed for shorter distances and are commonly used for interconnections between regional power grids. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing energy reliability and supporting renewable energy integration.</p></p>
