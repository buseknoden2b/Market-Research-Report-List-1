<p><p>The LED Visible Light Communication (VLC) system market is primarily divided into two types: PC-LED and RGB-LED. PC-LED, or pure white LEDs, are commonly used for lighting while facilitating data transmission through light modulation. In contrast, RGB-LEDs utilize red, green, and blue light combinations to transmit data, enabling more complex color patterns and higher data rates. Both types serve various applications, including smart lighting, indoor positioning, and high-speed data transfer, catering to the growing demand for efficient wireless communication.</p></p>
