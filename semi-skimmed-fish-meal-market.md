<p><ul><li>Flame Dried (FD) Fish Meal</li><li>Steam Dried (SD) Fish Meal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semi-skimmed fish meal market includes two primary types: Flame Dried (FD) and Steam Dried (SD) fish meal. FD fish meal is produced through direct exposure to flames, yielding a nutrient-dense product with high protein levels, making it ideal for aquaculture and livestock feed. In contrast, SD fish meal uses steam for drying, resulting in a more controlled processing environment that preserves amino acids and minimizes nutrient loss. Both types cater to different nutritional needs in animal feed formulations.</p></p>
