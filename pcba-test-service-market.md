<p><p>The PCBA test service market encompasses various testing methodologies to ensure circuit board reliability and functionality. In-Circuit Test (ICT) verifies individual components and connections, while Functional Circuit Test assesses the overall performance of the assembled board. Fatigue Testing evaluates long-term durability under stress, and Fixture Test involves using specialized fixtures for precise testing. Impedance Testing measures electrical resistance in circuits, ensuring optimal performance. Other tests may include thermal, environmental, or compliance testing, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
