<p><p>The magnesium oxide thermocouples market includes three main types: grounded, ungrounded, and exposed. Grounded types feature a direct connection to the measurement instrument, providing rapid response times, ideal for dynamic applications. Ungrounded types offer electrical isolation, minimizing interference from external signals, making them suitable for high-temperature environments. Exposed types are designed for applications requiring direct contact with the process, ensuring precise measurements in challenging conditions. Each type caters to diverse industrial needs based on response time, accuracy, and environmental factors.</p></p>
