<p><p>The sealed gauge transducer market comprises three key types based on pressure levels: low pressure sealed, mid pressure sealed, and high pressure sealed. Low pressure sealed transducers are designed for measuring pressures typically below 0.5 MPa, offering high accuracy for sensitive applications. Mid pressure sealed transducers cover a range between 0.5 to 3.5 MPa, suitable for various industrial processes. High pressure sealed transducers handle pressures over 3.5 MPa, essential for applications in oil and gas, aerospace, and hydraulic systems.</p></p>
