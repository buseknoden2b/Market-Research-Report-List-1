<p><ul><li>Absorption Grating</li><li>Phase Grating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-Ray grating market is largely categorized into two main types: absorption gratings and phase gratings. Absorption gratings utilize differing material absorption characteristics to disperse X-rays, making them effective for high-resolution imaging. In contrast, phase gratings rely on variations in the refractive index to manipulate X-ray waves, providing enhanced contrast and finer detail in imaging applications. Both types play crucial roles in fields like medical diagnostics, materials science, and research by improving the precision of X-ray analysis and imaging.</p></p>
