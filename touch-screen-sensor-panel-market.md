<p><ul><li>Capacitive</li><li>Resistive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The touch screen sensor panel market is primarily categorized into two types: capacitive and resistive. Capacitive screens use the electrical properties of the human body to detect touch, offering high clarity and multi-touch capabilities, ideal for smartphones and tablets. In contrast, resistive screens consist of multiple layers that respond to pressure, making them suitable for environments requiring durability and precision, such as industrial settings. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to diverse user preferences and operational needs.</p></p>
