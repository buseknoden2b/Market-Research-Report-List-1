<p><p>The spandex market is primarily categorized into solution dry spinning, solution wet spinning, and other methods. Solution dry spinning involves dissolving polyurethane in a solvent, then spinning it into fibers that solidify upon exposure to air. Solution wet spinning utilizes a coagulation bath to solidify the fibers, providing enhanced elasticity and strength. Other methods may include melt spinning or fiber modification techniques. Each type offers varying properties and applications, catering to diverse sectors such as textiles, apparel, and medical industries.</p></p>
