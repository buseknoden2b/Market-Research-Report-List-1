<p><p>The nickel recycling market comprises two main types: primary nickel and secondary nickel. Primary nickel is sourced directly from nickel mines and is essential for producing new nickel products. In contrast, secondary nickel is derived from recycled materials, such as scrap metal, which undergoes processes to recover nickel for reuse. The secondary nickel market offers environmental benefits by reducing waste and energy consumption, while ensuring a sustainable supply for various industries, including stainless steel manufacturing and battery production.</p></p>
