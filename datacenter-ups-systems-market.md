<p><p>The Datacenter UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) Systems market is primarily categorized into two battery types: lead-acid and lithium batteries. Lead-acid batteries are traditional, cost-effective, and reliable, suitable for short to medium backup times, but have limitations in lifespan and efficiency. Lithium batteries, on the other hand, offer longer lifespan, higher energy density, and quicker recharge times, making them ideal for modern datacenters requiring efficient, space-saving solutions. The choice between these batteries impacts performance, maintenance, and overall operational costs.</p></p>
