<p><p>The high precision tools market encompasses various specialized machines essential for precise manufacturing processes. Lathe machines shape materials by rotating them against cutting tools, while milling machines use rotating cutters to remove material from a workpiece. Drilling machines create holes with high accuracy, and grinding machines refine surfaces for smooth finishes. Boring machines expand existing holes to precise dimensions, and gear cutters are specifically designed to cut and shape gears with exact specifications, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
