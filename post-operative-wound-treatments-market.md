<p><p>The post-operative wound treatments market is primarily divided into advanced and traditional wound dressings. They are typically used for complex or chronic wounds. Traditional wound dressings, like gauze and adhesive bandages, offer basic protection but are less effective in managing moisture and preventing infection. The choice between these types depends on the wound's severity, location, and required care.</p></p>
