<p><strong>The Electrical Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Grain-oriented</li><li>Non Grain-oriented</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical steel market is primarily divided into two categories: grain-oriented and non-grain-oriented steel. Grain-oriented electrical steel is designed for high efficiency in transformers and motors by having a specific crystal orientation, which minimizes energy loss. Non-grain-oriented electrical steel, on the other hand, is used in applications where isotropic magnetic properties are essential, such as in electric motors and generators. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing energy efficiency in electrical applications.</p></p>
