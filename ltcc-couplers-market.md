<p><p>LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramics) couplers are essential components in microwave and RF applications, designed for different directional functionalities. </p><p>Directional couplers allow a signal to split, directing a portion to one output while sending the majority to another, making them ideal for monitoring or feedback purposes. </p><p>Dual-directional couplers enable signal separation in both directions, facilitating the connection between two ports while maintaining isolation, thus supporting bidirectional communication in complex circuits. Both types enhance performance and signal integrity in various applications.</p></p>
