<p><p>The PET preform making equipment market is categorized into three main types: hydraulic, electric, and hybrid. Hydraulic machines utilize oil to generate pressure, offering high speed and efficiency, making them suitable for large-scale production. Electric machines use electrical energy, promoting energy efficiency and precision, ideal for smaller batches. Hybrid machines combine the benefits of both hydraulic and electric systems, providing versatility and enhanced control. Each type serves distinct production needs based on scale, efficiency, and energy consumption requirements.</p></p>
