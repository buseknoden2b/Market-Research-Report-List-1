The market includes various types: </p><p>1. **LSH Morcellators** (Laparoscopic Suction Hydromechanical) are designed for cases requiring suction and fragmentation.</p><p>2. **LM Morcellators** (Laparoscopic Morcellators) focus on cutting and extracting tissue through small incisions.</p><p>3. **TLH Morcellators** (Total Laparoscopic Hysterectomy) are specialized for hysterectomy procedures.</p><p>4. **LAVH Morcellators** (Laparoscopic Assisted Vaginal Hysterectomy) assist in transvaginal tissue removal.</p><p>5. **Others** include specialized and alternative morcellation devices.</p></p>
