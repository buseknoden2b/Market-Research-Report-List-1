<p><p>The termite baiting treatments market primarily consists of two types: termite bait systems and termiticides. Termite bait systems involve strategically placed bait stations containing attractants and slow-acting insecticides that target termite colonies over time, effectively reducing their populations. Conversely, the termiticide market includes chemical treatments applied to soil and structures to create a barrier against termites, preventing infestations. Both approaches aim to protect properties from termite damage, with baiting being more environmentally friendly and termiticides offering immediate protection.</p></p>
